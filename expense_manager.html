Simple Expense Manager


Simple expense manager is a mobile app for managing your daily expenses.


Features


  • Add expense in minimum number of clicks
  • Add your own categories
  • Quick select option for frequently used categories
  • Custom start date
  • Graphical representation of your expenses

Why I built this app?


To manage my expenses. Few months back I moved from Goa to Hyderabad and my expenses started increasing drastically. I tried few expense manager apps, but I was not satisfied with any of them. I wanted something which would allow me to add expense quickly and at the same time display total expense/budget

Having knowledge of Mobile app development, I decided to develop an app which would cater to all my needs and at the same time would be very simple to use. For this, I decided used ionic framework which is powered by superheroic AngularJS.

After one month of development in my spare time, I was able to acheive what I wanted from an expense manager app.
